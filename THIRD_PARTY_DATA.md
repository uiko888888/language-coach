# Third-Party Data

Language Coach keeps imported dictionary data separate from user learning records. Imported datasets must retain their source, version, license, attribution, URL, and checksum.

## Open English WordNet

- Name: Open English WordNet
- Version: 2025
- Publisher: Global WordNet Association
- Project: https://github.com/globalwordnet/english-wordnet
- Release: https://en-word.net/static/english-wordnet-2025-json.zip
- License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)
- License URL: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

Canonical citation:

> John P. McCrae, Alexandre Rademaker, Francis Bond, Ewa Rudnicka and Christiane Fellbaum (2019). English WordNet 2019 - An Open-Source WordNet for English. Proceedings of the 10th Global WordNet Conference.

The Open English WordNet archive and generated SQLite data are not committed to this repository. The importer records the release checksum and attribution in `dictionary_sources`.
